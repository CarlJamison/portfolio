1000 Pound Club

The 1000-pound club is a achievement that takes most lifters multiple years of training to reach. It requires the lifters one rep max bench press, squat, and deadlift to total 1000 or more pounds. For most people this breaks down as around 225 Bench, 330 Squat, and 445 Deadlift, although it is different for each person. For women the equivalent is 600 pounds, where the break down will likely be closer to 120 Bench, 205 Squat, 275 Deadlift.

At our gym, you will receive prizes for hitting either of these milestones. Normal rules for the lift apply as well of course; squats need to be at least parallel; bar needs to touch your chest for bench etc. Happy lifting!
